Coach focusBiggest longevity lever

A VO₂ max of 34 is workable but below where we want you for your age. The good news: it's one of the most trainable numbers in this whole report, and small gains carry outsized longevity benefit.

  • Add two zone-2 sessions a week (45–60 min, conversational pace) to build your aerobic base.
  • Layer in one short interval session (e.g. 4×4 min hard) once the base is established.
  • Re-test VO₂ max at the next quarterly review - expect 3–5 points of movement.

This is a strong pillar - grip and lower-body power are excellent for your age. The one gap is upper-body pushing endurance. Keep this pillar as an anchor while we work the priority areas.
